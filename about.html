The story and vision of Ann Red

Ann Red tells her story through her own lens or with a drawing tool, which is quite fitting. With the use of analog film, analog cameras, developing her own photos and drawing her visions, she has managed to create and control her own destiny. "My work is often quite nostalgic both because the main part of my process is analog and because I tend to be a nostalgic person, dreaming of long summers and the internet and some took over the world that I just got to experience in my early childhood

.

I guess I always imagined that the world back in the days was more interesting and fulfilling and to this day I still wished I was alive in the 60s 70s 90s. 80s not so much (sorry not sorry). Inspired by music social movements and art of the time. Often people have told me that my art made them feel nostalgic even though I didn’t intended to."
